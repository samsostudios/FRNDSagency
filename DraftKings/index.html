Draft Kings is the game inside the game. In addition to season long leagues, they allow customers to compete in single-day online fantasy sports contests across the largest variety of professional sports. Draft Kings hosts watch parties and private events for their fans and customers around the U.S., fusing the love of the game with professional athletes, fans and FRNDS alike. Since the initial platform officially launched in 2012, DraftKings has grown explosively and has become a transformative experience, changing the way fans interact with sports.

Commercial

Content Creation

Draft Kings was looking to capture and advertise their VIP events to increase engagement, and involvement desire. Their unique experiences span from wineries to batting practice on Wrigley Field, or in an event space suped up with walls of LED screens to watch larger than life games. Due to the multitude of experiences and the range of services, our content has to be reactive and ever-evolving. We produce, art direct, shoot and edit on site event recap videos and photos used for sales and sponsorship promotions, and our content has landed prime time commercial spots on networks and in stadiums.
