Branding

Element Experiential approached us for a full rebrand, as a one stop shop for specialized event production. To balance their big, highly visual events we wanted to keep the branding clean and straightforward. We chose a typeface that is strong, durable and modern; creating a logo that is unapologetically bold and unmistakably clear, with a flare of creativity.

Web Design

02

The goal for online is to create an uncomplicated, responsive website where the visual content drives the talking.

The website design matches the new brand aesthetic - it is confident, powerful and determined. Mesmerizing visual content consisting of photos and videos drives engagement, draws visitors, and shares the stories behind each event and production.

Content Creation

With big events, comes big moments. Element Experiential has engaged us to produce, capture and edit all visual content to showcase their client projects around the world. Capturing impactful moments in dynamic ways, we help to give visual insight into Element’s story behind the scenes and on the front lines.
